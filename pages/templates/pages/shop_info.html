Welcome to the Allan E Hewitt Gallery!

Celebrating the fusion of art and earth

Our shop offers a curated collection of captivating landscape paintings and lovingly crafted soil profiles that cater to both art enthusiasts and scientists alike.

In a unique collaboration between artistry and scientific inquiry, the work of Allan E Hewitt explores landscapes, techincal soil profiles, and the odd experimentation!

Soil Profiles: An exclusive collection of hand-drawn soil profiles. Recognized and published in scientific publications and websites, the profiles unveil the hidden layers of the earth, showcasing the diverse and complex composition of soils in loving detail. Merging scientific precision with artistic finesse, these soil profiles serve as both educational tools and captivating pieces of art.

Landscapes: A collection demontrating a love of the land in it's surface format, and particularly the artist's love of the South Island of New Zealand, landscape painting was the artist's favoured personal art format.

Other: Forays into impressionism, watercolours, and even using soil as a painting medium, are collected for your pleasure and purchase!

Shop our collection and bring home a piece of nature's magnificence while supporting the convergence of art and science.
